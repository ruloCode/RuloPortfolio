Santa Marta had never hosted a hackathon. Ever. So when the **Primera Hackatón Samatech** was announced — 70 builders, teams of 5, 30 hours, +50M COP in prizes and benefits — I knew I had to be there.

This is the story of those 30 hours.

## A city putting tech on the table

What made this event special wasn't just the format. It was what it meant: for the first time, technology was at the center of the city's most important conversations. More than 20 mentors traveled from all over Colombia to guide us. Sponsors believed in the event before it was anything more than an idea. Volunteers gave up their weekend out of love for their city.

You could feel it in the room: this wasn't just a competition. It was Santa Marta saying *we build here too*.
