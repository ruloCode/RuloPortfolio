## The event

The **Primera Hackatón Samatech** was a milestone for Santa Marta: the first hackathon in the city's history. The numbers tell the story of its ambition:

- **70 builders** selected to participate
- **Teams of 5**, formed to mix skills and backgrounds
- **30 hours** of non-stop building
- **+50M COP** in prizes and benefits
- **20+ mentors** who traveled from all over Colombia to guide the teams

More than a competition, it was a statement: technology has a seat at the table in Santa Marta's future.

Thirty hours is not enough time to build something perfect — and that's exactly the point. A hackathon forces you to make the trade-offs that senior engineering is really about: scope ruthlessly, ship the core, and communicate what matters in the pitch.
